How Fertile is the US?

A Brief Glance at the Fertility Rate of the 50 US States

Among the 50 states in the United States, are there any interesting trends in fertility? Taking CDC data from 2018, a basic color-coded map was prooduced to see if there were any visible trends of note. From the data, South Dakota had the highest rate with 73.6, while Vermont was the lowest with 47.2.

It would be interesting to break the rates down by age group or race per state. Also, do other factors like religion or community culture contribute to the data? If so, how?

Hover over a state in the map below to see the fertility rate and number of births for that state.
